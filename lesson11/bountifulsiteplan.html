Conners Bountiful Foods Site Plan

My Site Purpose

The Bountiful Foods website aims to provide a platform for customers to purchase organic and healthy food products while also educating them about the importance of a healthy lifestyle and sustainable food practices. The website also aims to promote the brand’s image and values while increasing sales.

Target Audience

The target audience for Bountiful Foods website are health-conscious individuals who are interested in buying organic and healthy food products. They are generally in the age range of 25 to 45, have higher income levels, and are willing to pay a premium for quality and sustainably produced food products. They are also environmentally conscious and are interested in reducing their carbon footprint.

Dieters

These are individuals who are currently on a dieting program or who are seeking to lose weight. We will appeal to these individuals by advartising the organic, healthy, and fresh aspects of the food we are selling.

Color Scheme

The color scheme for Bountiful Foods website will be based on green and earthy tones to reflect the brand’s focus on sustainability and organic products.
