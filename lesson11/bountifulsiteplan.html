Conners Bountiful Foods Site Plan

My Site Purpose

The Bountiful Foods website aims to provide a platform for customers to purchase organic and healthy food products while also educating them about the importance of a healthy lifestyle and sustainable food practices. The website also aims to promote the brand’s image and values while increasing sales.

Target Audience

The target audience for Bountiful Foods website are Health Enthusiast "health-conscious" individuals who are interested in buying organic and healthy food products. They are generally in the age range of 25 to 45, have higher income levels, and are willing to pay a premium for quality and sustainably produced food products. They are also environmentally conscious and are interested in reducing their carbon footprint.

Health Enthusiast

A health enthusiast is someone who is passionate about promoting and maintaining a healthy lifestyle. They prioritize physical fitness, proper nutrition, and mental well-being in their daily routine. They are often avid consumers of health-related information and seek out the latest research and trends in the field. They may also participate in activities such as yoga, meditation, or sports to improve their overall health. Health enthusiasts may also encourage others to adopt healthier habits and may even pursue careers in fields related to health and wellness.

Color Scheme

The color scheme for Bountiful Foods website will be based on green and earthy tones to reflect the brand’s focus on sustainability and organic products.
