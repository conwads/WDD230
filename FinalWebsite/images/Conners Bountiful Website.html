    • San Diego

    San Diego California is known for having the best weather in the world. Average Temperatures stay around 70 degrees Fahrenheit and there is roughly 260 days of sunshine each year. This leads to some of the best and freshest fruit the US has to offer. Other than fruit, the city of San Diego is home to over 1.3 million people which is no surpise when you consider that it's located on the coast of the Pacific Ocean. On the coast are some of the most beautiful beaches in the world, including La Jolla Cove and Coronado Beach. San Diego is home to the largest urban park in the United States, Balboa Park, which spans 1,200 acres and features 15 museums, several theaters, and the world-famous San Diego Zoo.
